DWPF: Dark Web Pharma Framework (Investigative Tool)

How to use the DWPF Tool


1. The tool has four (4) steps. Click on the DWFP circle to explore the steps.
2. Navigate to Step 1.
`` Click on your choice of software to download and install. Eg. TOR
`` In your TOR browser navigate to step 2.
3. In Step 2,
`` Plan accordingly and have keywords you want to begin your search with.
`` Click on Google Dorking to explore and utilize Google Dorking. Eg. “Covid-19” AND “Products”.
`` Navigate to and click on the search engine circle to explore options. Eg Ahmia.
`` Click on Covid-19 Vaccine Darkmarkets to Check out the already existing markets on vaccine procurement.
`` Review any identified findings from step 2.
4. Navigate to step 3.
`` Explore tools like Maltego to check out or process any pieces of evidence or intelligence gathered.
5. Navigate to step 4.
`` Explore additional investigative tools.

Notes

This Dark Web Pharma Framework (DWPF) Investigative Tool was created using the open-source OSINT Framework by Justin Nordine (https://github.com/lockfale/osint-framework).
DWPIT leverages the OSINT framework from several free sources and tools resources to aid investigators as they conduct investigations relating to the dark web.
Specifically, COVID-19 product being either advertised or sold on the Dark Web. Ultimately, the goal is to help investigators navigate the Dark Web with these free Tool.
